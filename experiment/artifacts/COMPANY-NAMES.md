# Company Name Ideas

## Direction: Personal, warm, home-based AI
- **Hearth** — it lives in your home, it's warm, it's private, it gathers the family
- **Domus** — Latin for home. Clean, technical
- **Kept** — your AI is kept local, kept safe, kept private
- **Kin** — family-oriented, adaptive AI that knows your household
- **Hearthstone** — the central place in the home
- **NestMind** — too Google? but clear
- **Homebrew AI** — casual, hacker-culture
- **Private** — just "Private" — bold, one word
- **Lodge** — homey, private gathering place
- **Vault** — privacy-first, but maybe too security-heavy
- **Local** — straightforward: "Local AI"

## Direction: Technical, model-focused
- **Forge** — where models are shaped and distilled
- **Distill** — literal, from the distillation process
- **Aperture** — focused, narrow, private
- **Lens** — clear view, personal, focused
- **Prism** — takes light and reveals its spectrum. Distillation metaphor.

## Direction: Abstract/brandable
- **Oura-like single words:** Vell, Thyme, Coda, Luma, Sol, Nura
- **Combos:** LocalMind, HomeAI, HearthAI, KinAI
- **Latin/Greek:** Sophia (wisdom), Domus (home), Oikos (household), Phronesis (practical wisdom)

## Top picks:
1. **Hearth** — most evocative. "Hearth lives in your home. It knows your family. Nothing leaves."
2. **Kin** — short, warm. "Your family AI."
3. **Oikos** (oy-kos) — household in Greek. Unique, ownable domain.
4. **Vell** — clean, abstract. "Your local AI."
